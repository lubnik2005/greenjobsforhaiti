Composting

Buy organic and green-waste trash from any and everyone who goes and finds it and brings it to collection centers. Then it will be proccessed into compost, and sold cheaply to local farmers. Go somewhere

Irrigation

Some statistics show rainfall at 50” per year in 2 rainy seasons, but with rainy seasons come dry seasons. Another industry that JACHOB will start is going around and building large ponds close to growing fields where rain can run into, and then using gravity, feed water to the fields. Learn More

Animal Feed

Haiti cannot afford to grow animals. Animal feed is too expensive for farmers to grow animals except to let them wander the fields and eat what they can. After Jachob removes the vegetable oil from the seeds the remainder of the seed is called seedcake and can be used for animal feed. Learn More
